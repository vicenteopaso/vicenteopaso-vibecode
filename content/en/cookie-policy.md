## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Some are essential for security or website functionality; others (not used here) track behavior for analytics or advertising.

## Cookies Used on This Site

opa.so uses **only one category** of cookie:

### 1. Strictly Necessary Security Cookie (Cloudflare Turnstile)

- Purpose: Verify legitimate visitors and block bots.
- Example cookie: `cf_clearance`
- Personal data processed: minimal technical data required for verification.
- Necessity: Required for site security and functionality.
- Legal basis: **Legitimate Interest** (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

### No Analytics, Marketing, or Tracking Cookies

We do not use Google Analytics, marketing trackers, or any third-party advertising cookies.

## Cookie Management

The Turnstile cookie is essential for bot protection and cannot be disabled without breaking website functionality.
