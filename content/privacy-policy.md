# Privacy Policy for opa.so

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how opa.so ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data when you visit https://opa.so/. We comply with the EU GDPR, the Spanish AEPD (“Agencia Española de Protección de Datos”), and applicable data-protection regulations.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Data You Provide Voluntarily

When you submit the Contact form, we collect:

- Your email address
- Your message content
- Any additional information you voluntarily include

The form is processed by Formspree, our third-party data processor.

### 2. Security & Anti-abuse Data (Cloudflare Turnstile)

To protect the website from bots and malicious traffic, Cloudflare Turnstile processes:

- IP address
- Browser characteristics
- Interaction data required to verify that you are human

Cloudflare acts as an independent controller for this processing.

### 3. Hosting, Logs & Traffic Data (Vercel)

Because the site is deployed on Vercel, standard hosting logs and edge analytics are automatically collected. These may include:

- IP address
- Timestamp of the request
- URL paths visited
- Device, operating system, and browser type
- Referrer information
- Basic performance and routing metrics (Edge Network telemetry)

Vercel uses this data for:

- Security
- Abuse prevention
- Performance monitoring
- Error diagnostics

Vercel acts as an independent controller.  
Learn more: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

### 4. No Tracking or Advertising Cookies

We do **not** use analytics tools that track identifiable behavior, nor do we use advertising cookies.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Contact form data** is used only to respond to your inquiries.
- **Cloudflare and Vercel data** is used for security, abuse prevention, and website performance.
- We do not sell or share your data for marketing or advertising purposes.

## Lawful Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We rely on:

- **Legitimate Interest:** Responding to inquiries and ensuring website security and stability.
- **Consent:** Provided explicitly when you submit the contact form. You may withdraw your consent at any time.

## Third-Party Data Processors

- **Formspree** — processes contact form submissions securely.  
  https://formspree.io/security/
- **Cloudflare** — processes security and anti-abuse data as an independent controller.  
  https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/
- **Vercel** — processes hosting logs and edge analytics as an independent controller.  
  https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

## Data Transfers

Formspree and Vercel may process data in the United States or other jurisdictions.  
Transfers are protected using Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other GDPR-compliant safeguards.

## Data Retention

- Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to you.
- Hosting logs (Vercel) and anti-abuse data (Cloudflare) follow each provider’s retention policies.
- We do not maintain local or persistent databases of visitor information.

## Your Rights

You may request to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent
- Request portability of data you provided

To exercise your rights, email: **contact@opa.so**

You may also file a complaint with the AEPD:  
https://www.aepd.es/
